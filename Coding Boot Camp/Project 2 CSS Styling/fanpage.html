Amanda Palmer


Who Is Amanda Palmer?

Amanda Palmer is a singer/songwriter, actor, stage performer & author.

Her hometown is Boston, Massachusetts. Palmer's interest in theatre influences her songwriting, which is described as, "punk cabaret". Palmer previously spent time as a street performer, prior to meeting Brian Viglione, in 2000. Together, the two formed The Dresden Dolls

After successful experiences in The Dresden Dolls, Palmer branched off into solo and other projects.
